        About us

        Created by a small group of visionary indie hotel owners and operators, Curator Hotel & Resort Collection unites hotels who value flexibility and freedom of choice—but also want a competitive edge. Curator was launched in November 2020 with a mission to improve the operating performance and profitability of small brands and independent lifestyle properties without compromising what makes them unique.
